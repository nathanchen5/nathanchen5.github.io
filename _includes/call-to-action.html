Year 1

Freshman year felt rough. I hadn’t planned on being engineer, and yet I found myself in the incredibly privileged position of being directly admitted to UW CS. The result was continuous self-doubt, half from feeling out of my depth and half to talk myself out of the major: “Do I even belong here?” and “Everyone knows more than me,” ad infinitum.

Amid this angst, I decided to rail against my uncertainty by doing what any confused college kid might do—furiously committing myself to every opportunity besides CS itself.
