Onwards

After graduation, I intend to continue building upon the “community engineering” that I discovered at UW. To start, I’ll be working as a software engineer at an EdTech company, where I’ll continue to build resources for learners across the globe. And while not a student, I plan to remain active in the classroom—you can expect to see me sharing my CS experiences, joining alumni panels, and volunteering to give K-12 kids their first tastes of computer science. Then, when I’ve had my fill of engineering, I hope to come full circle and become a professor, guiding students’ self-discovery from the other side.

With luck, that’ll be a story in itself.
